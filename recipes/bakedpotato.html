How To: Baked Potato!

Simple Baked Potato

A easy-to-follow guide on how to make rich and creamy baked potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 1 baking potato
  • Aluminum foil

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Scrub potato then wrap with aluminum foil. Prick the wrapped potato with a fork to prevent steam from building up and causing the potato to explode in your oven.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake for 1 1/2 hours.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving!

  • Calories: 128
  • Protein: 2.7g
  • Carbohydrates: 29.7g
  • Fat: 0.1g

This recipe yields 1 serving with the cook time of an hour and a half.
